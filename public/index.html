About Us

Shaping Tomorrow's Leaders Since Day One

Nirmaan Public School is a premier English-medium institution located at Aliganj Bazar, Sultanpur, Uttar Pradesh. We follow the National Curriculum, offering comprehensive education from Playgroup all the way to Grade 10.

Our motto — "विद्या ददाति विनयम्" (Education gives humility) — reflects our belief that true education shapes character, not just careers. We create a safe, nurturing environment where every child is seen, heard, and celebrated.
