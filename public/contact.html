Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We've probably answered it below. If not, feel free to send us a message above.

When do admissions for 2026-27 close?

Admissions for the 2026-27 academic session are open now, but seats are limited. We strongly recommend applying at the earliest to secure your child's spot. Walk-in enquiries are also welcome during office hours.

Is there an entrance test for admission?

For Playgroup through Grade 5, there is no formal entrance test — just a friendly interaction session. For Grades 6–10, a basic subject assessment may be conducted to ensure appropriate grade placement.

What is the medium of instruction?

Nirmaan Public School follows full English medium instruction from Playgroup through Grade 10. Hindi is also taught as a core subject across all grades as per the National Curriculum.

What curriculum does NPS follow?

We follow the National Curriculum framework, providing comprehensive, balanced education that meets national educational standards while incorporating modern teaching methodologies and smart classroom technology.

Is transportation facility available?

Please contact our school office at 991-822-5511 for the most up-to-date information on transportation routes and availability in your area.

Are co-curricular activities part of the school program?

Absolutely! Co-curricular activities including sports, arts, music, dance, debates, and cultural events are an integral part of the NPS experience. We believe in holistic all-round development beyond academics alone.

How safe is the campus?

Student safety is our top priority. Our campus features CCTV surveillance throughout, trained security personnel at entry points, child-safe infrastructure, and strict visitor protocols to ensure every child is safe every day.

Can I visit the school before applying?

We warmly welcome school visits! Parents are encouraged to visit us during office hours (Mon–Sat, 8 AM – 3 PM) to take a campus tour, meet our faculty, and get all your questions answered in person. No appointment needed.
