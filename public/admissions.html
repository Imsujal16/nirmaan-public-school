How to Apply

Simple 4-Step Admission Process

Getting your child admitted to NPS is quick and straightforward. Follow these four simple steps and we'll take care of the rest.

1

Fill the Enquiry Form

Complete the online enquiry form below or visit our school office in person. Provide basic details about your child and the grade you're applying for.

2

Submit Documents

Bring the required documents to our admission office — birth certificate, previous school report card, and two passport-size photos.

3

Interaction Session

A brief, friendly interaction with our academic team helps us understand your child's interests and learning style. No entrance test for early grades.

4

Confirm Admission

Receive your admission confirmation, pay the applicable fees, and your child officially becomes a proud member of the NPS family!

Requirements

Eligibility & Documents Required

Here's everything you need to know about admission eligibility and the documents to bring on your visit.

Eligibility Criteria

Child must meet the minimum age requirement for the applied grade (as of April 1, 2026)
Students applying for Grade 2 and above must produce the previous year's report card
No formal entrance exam for Playgroup to Grade 5 — just a friendly interaction
Grades 6–10 may require a basic subject assessment to ensure proper grade placement
Parents must attend a brief orientation session before final confirmation of admission

For detailed fee structure and scholarship information, please visit the school office or call us at 991-822-5511. We believe quality education should be accessible to all.

Documents to Submit

Birth Certificate of the child (Original + 1 Photocopy)
4 Recent Passport-Size Photographs of the child
Previous School Report Card / Transfer Certificate (for Grade 2+)
Parent / Guardian Aadhar Card (Photocopy)
Address Proof (Aadhar / Voter ID / Utility Bill)
Medical / Vaccination Record (optional, encouraged)
