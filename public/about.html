Our Story

A Legacy of Learning
in the Heart of Sultanpur

Nirmaan Public School was founded with a singular vision — to bring quality, English-medium education to the families of Sultanpur and surrounding communities. Located at Aliganj Bazar, Sultanpur (U.P.) 227805, our campus has grown from a small school into a thriving institution serving hundreds of students.

We follow the National Curriculum, offering comprehensive education from Playgroup through Grade 10. Our modern campus features smart classrooms, dedicated activity spaces, and a safe, nurturing environment where every child can flourish.

Our Core Values

The Pillars That Define Us

Everything we do at Nirmaan Public School is guided by four core values that shape our culture, our classrooms, and our community.

Character Building

We instill integrity, discipline, empathy, and moral values that shape students into responsible citizens and compassionate human beings.

Holistic Development

Academic excellence meets creative expression, physical fitness, and social skills — ensuring every child grows in mind, body, and spirit.

Safe & Nurturing Environment

CCTV surveillance, trained security, and a child-safe campus ensure your child is protected while learning in a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

Academic Excellence

A rigorous National Curriculum, smart classrooms, and activity-based learning drive exceptional results and a lifelong love for knowledge.
